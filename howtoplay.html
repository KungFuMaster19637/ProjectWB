How To Play

1. To enter the leaderboard, you will have to record a video of your run and upload it to YouTube.

2. The run has to be in 1 go. You can reset the floor if you want to but you can't reset the whole run by exiting abyss and redoing a chamber for energy, that would count as a seperate run.

3. After recording and uploading your run, send a link to the appropiate channel.

3a. If you have the time for it, also put your weapons used, your character cons and cleartimes in the description of the video or in the channel itself.

3b. If you know how the penalty system works you could also calculate for yourself what your total clear time is, I would appreciate it.

4. The leaderboard will be ranked on the following criteria:
a. Stars
b. Total clear time
c. Points used
Example: Player 1 cleared with 9 stars and total clear time 7:58 and used 60 points, Player 2 cleared with 8 stars and total clear time 7:15 and used 58 points. Player 1 will be ranked above Player 2 due to stars being more important than clear time and points used.
