General Rules

1. The challenges will be played on floor 12 only .

2. Every 4 star character is allowed. Even if you don't have a certain character c6, it's hard to balance them per constellation.

3. Every 5 star standard banner character constellation is allowed. As these characters are usually not gaining huge increases in powerlevel, combined with the rng aspect to get them in the first place, I will not put any penalties on using them.

4. Every 5 star limited banner character constellation will get penalties depending on how many constellations you have on that character. For every constellation you have above c0, your clear times will be increased by 3 seconds.

4a. The following characters receive another 3 second penalty upon using them: Hu Tao c1, Ayaka c2, Kazuha c2, Eula c6, Shenhe c6, Ganyu c6, Yelan c6.

4b. The following character receive another 20 second penalty upon using them: Raiden c2, Nahida c2.

5. Every player has access to 2 5 star weapons . To keep things balanced, every refinement above r1 will have a 3 second penalty.

5a. Battlepass weapons are allowed, but if the refinement of the weapon is higher than r3, it will have a 3 second penalty

Walmart Bets Rules

1. Everyone will start with a certain budget. The budget will change each cycle and will be displayed at the shop.

2. The player will then use their budget to buy up to 8 characters from the shop and add them to their team. The cost of the shop units are determined by their performance and value from general knowledge.

3. You are not allowed to overspend on points. Underspending on points however, will reward the player with a reduction of 5 seconds of their total clear time per point not used.
